Legal Guidance FAQs

Common questions about navigating legal processes in India

How does your platform help me file an FIR?

We provide a step-by-step guide per Section 154 of CrPC, including:
- Required documents checklist
- Template for written complaints
- Jurisdiction mapping for police stations
- Your rights during FIR filing

How do you determine correct court jurisdiction?

Our system calculates jurisdiction based on:
1. Case type (civil/criminal/family)
2. Location of property/parties (Section 16-20 CPC)
3. Monetary value (for civil cases)
We cross-reference with the Indian Courts Jurisdiction Act

Are your legal templates legally valid?

All templates are:
- Drafted by practicing advocates
- Verified against Supreme Court formats
- Regularly updated per latest amendments
Note: Always consult a lawyer before final submission

How current is your legal information?

We maintain accuracy through:
- Daily updates from India Code portal
- SC/HC judgment monitoring
- Partnership with Bar Council of India
- User update requests via feedback

Can I directly file cases through this platform?

We provide:
- Preparation guidance (forms/documents)
- Court fee calculators
- Filing procedure walkthroughs
Note: Physical filing must be done at designated courts as per CPC

How are lawyers verified on your platform?

Our attorney directory includes:
- Bar Council registration numbers
- Specialization certificates
- Peer/client reviews
- Minimum 5 years practice requirement
- Regular license validity checks
